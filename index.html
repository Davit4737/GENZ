Speaking Rubric (0–4 per task)

  • 4 — clear, organized, relevant, and mostly accurate language use
  • 3 — generally effective, but with noticeable weaknesses
  • 2 — limited development, frequent language problems
  • 1 — very weak or incomplete response
  • 0 — no response or unrelated response

Writing Rubric (0–5)

  • 5 — well organized, clear opinion, effective support, strong language control
  • 4 — good response with some noticeable language or organization issues
  • 3 — adequate meaning, but weak structure or frequent errors
  • 2 — limited development and substantial language problems
  • 1–0 — minimal, unclear, or missing response
